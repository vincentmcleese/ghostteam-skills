## KEY PATTERNS ACROSS BOTH

### Hook Types That Work
1. **Contrarian:** "X doesn't die. It EXPLODES."
2. **Personal observation:** "ive been hanging out with..."
3. **Underestimation:** "I don't think people grasp..."
4. **Bold claim:** "claude code will probably make 10,000 people millionaires"
5. **Pattern naming:** "a huge number of ai products feel impressive for 48 hours and then..."
6. **How-to:** "how to [specific outcome]"
7. **Numbered list tease:** "10 skills to turn Claude Code into..."

### Structure That Works
1. One sentence per line
2. Specific numbers always ($200k, 48 hours, 99%, 10,000)
3. Real tool names (Claude, ChatGPT, Obsidian, n8n)
4. Lowercase for casual tone
5. Pop culture references people recognize
6. End with punch (memorable closer or question)
7. White space between ideas

### Tone That Works
1. Anti-gatekeeping energy
2. "I did this" not "you should do this"
3. Acknowledge nuance without hedging the main point
4. Excited but not hype-y
5. Mentor talking to peers, not guru to followers
6. Self-aware about being "boring" or obvious when relevant
