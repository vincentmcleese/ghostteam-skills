## Lead Magnet ‚Äî Deep Dive {#lead-magnet}

**Frequency**: 23.5% of posts | **Avg engagement**: 1,741 | **Purpose**: Lead generation

### Structure
```
[HOOK ‚Äî Any hook type works]
[VALUE DEMONSTRATION ‚Äî Show what the tool/system does]
[USE CASES ‚Äî 15-25 across 3-5 categories with ‚Üí arrows]
[IMPACT STATEMENT ‚Äî "The craziest part?"]
[CTA ‚Äî "Want the [guide/template/setup]?" + "Comment '[KEYWORD]'"]
```

### Length: 250-350 words | 80% have video

### Hook Variations

**Hook 1: Value-first**
```
[Impressive result or capability statement]

[Brief explanation of what it does]
```
Examples:
- "So... I now have a $50k/month market research agency, directly in my Claude.\n\nSpy on competitors, do market analysis and get customer insights - all in minutes."
- "I just automated my entire SEO workflow with AI.\n\nWebsite analysis, keyword research, content optimization - all in one chat."
- "I built a complete content engine in Claude.\n\n1 idea ‚Üí 50 pieces of content in 10 minutes."

**Hook 2: Problem-solution**
```
Most [people/companies] are still [old painful way].

Meanwhile, I [new better way].
```
Examples:
- "Most companies are still paying $50k/month for market research.\n\nMeanwhile, I do it in Claude for <$100/month."
- "Most marketers are still manually creating content.\n\nMeanwhile, I automated the entire pipeline with AI."

**Hook 3: Curiosity**
```
I just finished building [impressive thing].

Here's what it can do:
```
Examples:
- "I just finished building a complete market research system in Claude.\n\nHere's what it can do:"
- "I just finished building a $15k/month SEO agency in Claude.\n\nHere's what it can do:"

### CTA Pattern (REQUIRED)
```
Want the [guide/template/setup/playbook]?

- Comment '[KEYWORD]'
- I'll send you access (connect so I can DM)

I'll DM you everything

Happy building
```

**Frequency**: 2.0% of posts | **Avg engagement**: 3,638 ‚≠êÔ∏è HIGHEST | **Purpose**: Viral reach, thought leadership

### Structure
```
[HOOK ‚Äî Bold claim, contrarian take, or excitement about AI development]
[WHAT'S HAPPENING ‚Äî Brief context on the news/trend]
[WHY IT MATTERS ‚Äî 3-5 implications with ‚Üí arrows]
```

### Length: 200-300 words | NO CTA | 100% of top posts have video

### Hook Variations

**Hook 1: "AI is going bananas" + announcement**
```
AI is going bananas...

[Company] just released [feature] & it's [impact statement]
```

**Hook 2: "Unpopular opinion" contrarian**
```
Unpopular opinion: [contrarian statement that challenges common belief]
```

**Hook 3: Bold prediction**
```
[Bold prediction about the future]

Here's why:
```

### Key Rules
- NO CTA. This is pure engagement content.
- Make it genuinely contrarian ‚Äî not "AI is great" but "Most AI agents are just fancy chatbots with extra steps"
- Include specific examples to back up the take
- End with a strong, memorable statement

## Cost Transformation ‚Äî Deep Dive {#cost-transformation}

**Frequency**: Multiple posts | **Avg engagement**: 2,665 ‚≠êÔ∏è | **Purpose**: Demonstrate value, attract cost-conscious audience

### Structure
```
[HOOK ‚Äî Before/after cost comparison]
[OLD WAY ‚Äî What companies currently pay/do]
[NEW WAY ‚Äî How AI replaces it]
[HOW IT WORKS ‚Äî 3-5 steps or capabilities]
[USE CASES ‚Äî Industry-specific applications]
[ROI STATEMENT ‚Äî Final cost comparison]
```

### Length: 250-300 words | 80% have video

### Hook Variations

**Hook 1: Classic before/after**
```
[Service] used to cost $[high amount]/month [old way]

Now it's $[low amount] [new way]
```

**Hook 2: ROI focus**
```
Most companies pay $[amount]/month for [service].

I do it for $[much lower amount].

Same results. [X]x less cost.
```

**Hook 3: Time + cost savings**
```
[Service]:
‚Ä¢ Old way: $[amount]/month, [time] of work
‚Ä¢ New way: $[lower amount]/month, [less time] of work

[X]x cheaper. [Y]x faster.
```

### Key Rules
- ALWAYS use specific dollar amounts ‚Äî never vague ("expensive" ‚Üí "$15k/month")
- Include the multiplier ("150x cheaper", "40x faster")
- Name the old way AND the new way specifically
- Show what tools enable the transformation

## Multi-Type Strategy {#multi-type}

The highest-performing posts combine multiple types. When writing, identify ALL relevant types and create versions for each.

**Example**: The "$50k/month market research agency" post (8,864 engagement) was simultaneously:
- **Lead Magnet** (has comment CTA)
- **Tool Announcement** (Apify MCP release)
- **Tutorial** (step-by-step with emoji numbers)
- **Cost Transformation** (implied $50k ‚Üí $100)

**Strategy**: When a topic fits 3+ types, the Lead Magnet version will likely perform best because it combines value demonstration with engagement mechanics.

## Expansion Strategy {#expansion}

### Turning a Simple Idea into a Full Post

**From**: "AI can do market research now"

**Expand to**:
1. **Specific tool**: Claude + Apify MCP
2. **Specific cost**: $50k/month agency ‚Üí <$100/month
3. **Specific use cases**: Competitor analysis, review scraping, lead scoring, sentiment analysis, pricing intelligence
4. **Specific steps**: 1Ô∏è‚É£ Configure MCP ‚Üí 2Ô∏è‚É£ Prompt Claude ‚Üí 3Ô∏è‚É£ Generate report
5. **Specific results**: "Interactive HTML dashboard with charts and insights"
6. **Impact statement**: "Everything that used to require entire departments now happens in one conversation"

### Use Case Expansion Formula

Start with 3-5 obvious use cases, then expand by asking:
- What other industries need this?
- What other departments could use it?
- What adjacent workflows does it enable?
- What manual processes does it replace?

Target 15-25 use cases across 3-5 categories for Lead Magnet posts.
